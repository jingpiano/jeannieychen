My Teaching

  We are all learners, and we are all still learning.

Building inclusive teaching environments means respecting that each individual may have different learning styles and starting points.

Teaching can be understood as a continuous active learning process.

This WI+RE Learning Narrative articulates my firsthand experience as a volunteer at a rural school in Tianshangping (天上平), located in Hubei Province (湖北), China. I was invited to be part of a special team of teachers and school administrators from the Wuhan Foreign Languages School (WFLS), a K-12 academy in central China.

  Learners can be teachers, and teachers can be learners.

Instruction can be a two-way street.
