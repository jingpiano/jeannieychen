Youth Ambassador Profile



Purpose Statement

The issue I chose to address with my project is the lack of youth involvement in civic issues with a specific focus on youth voting.

In 2018 youth voting reached its peak, nearly doubling the percentage of voters in 2014, however, that peak was only 28% of youth. Youth voters in the 2020 election will make up nearly 40% of the vote and could have a huge impact on the election's turnout.

My club, SOCE (Students Organizing Civic Engagement), aims to combat youth apathy to civic issues in our meetings by teaching students at our school the importance of both voting and simply becoming involved in the community. The goal of my project is to gain more members in my club.
